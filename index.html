New blog spot

After spending time on other blog sites like blogger and wordpress I found it more difficult to keep up with linking my interests properly. So, instead of setting up a complicated database driven solution I felt it was okay to just go native and write it all in html.

That's right. I am foregoing the traditional approach and just blogging to blog. No link backs, no comments, no fancy polls, just code. However, I'm not closed off to questions or other responses so feel free to open an issue about a post and the discussion can live on there forever.
